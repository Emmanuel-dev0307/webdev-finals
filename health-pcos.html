PCOS

Description: Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a common hormonal disorder affecting individuals of reproductive age. It is characterized by an imbalance in reproductive hormones, specifically elevated levels of androgens (male-pattern hormones), which can interfere with the regular release of eggs from the ovaries.

Common Causes: Excess insulin, low-grade inflammation, heredity, excess androgen.

PCOS Symptoms:
  • Irregular Period
  • Excess Androgen
  • Polycystic Ovaries
  • Weight Struggles
  • Acanthosis Nigricans

Treatment: Lifestyle changes, hormonal birth control, metformin, fertility treatments if needed.

When To See A Doctor: Early diagnosis and treatment are crucial to managing symptoms and reducing the risk of long-term complications like Type 2 diabetes and heart disease.

Prevention: Maintain a healthy weight, exercise regularly, eat a low-glycemic diet.
