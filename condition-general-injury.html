Fracture

Description: A fracture is a break or crack in a bone caused by strong force, pressure, or injury.

Common Causes: Falls, vehicle accidents, sports injuries, heavy impacts, or weakened bones.

Symptoms: Severe pain, swelling, bruising, deformity, difficulty moving the affected area, and tenderness.

Treatment: Immobilization with a cast or splint, rest, ice, pain medication, and surgery in severe cases.

When to See a Doctor: Immediately seek medical attention if the bone appears deformed, pain is severe, or movement is impossible.

Prevention: Wear protective gear, maintain strong bones with proper nutrition, and avoid unsafe activities.
