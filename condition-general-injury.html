General Injury

Description: A general injury refers to any physical harm or damage to the body caused by an external force or accident.

Common Causes: Accidents, falls, sports, workplace incidents, physical altercations.

Symptoms: Pain, swelling, bruising, bleeding, restricted movement depending on area affected.

Treatment: First aid, rest, ice, compression, elevation, seek medical care for serious injuries.

When to See a Doctor: If bleeding does not stop, pain is severe, or there is suspected bone or organ damage.

Prevention: Follow safety guidelines, use protective equipment, be aware of surroundings.
