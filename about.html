About Us

SYMPTOMS IDENTIFIER

We are BSCpE 2A, a group of Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering students who are making a website for developing a reliable and user-friendly platform focused on health awareness and education.


This website is designed to assist individuals who engage in self-assessment by providing structured and accessible information on a wide range of health-related topics. These include common illnesses and injuries, as well as specialized areas such as menstruation, menopause, polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS), breast health, prostate health, testosterone-related concerns, and hair loss.


Our objective is to support users in recognizing possible symptoms, improving their understanding of various conditions, and encouraging informed decision-making regarding their health. By presenting information in a clear and organized manner, we aim to promote early awareness and guide users on when it is appropriate to seek professional medical consultation.


This platform is developed strictly for educational purposes. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and should not be used as a substitute for consultation with qualified healthcare professionals.


As students of BSCpE 2A, this project reflects our dedication to applying our technical knowledge in creating meaningful solutions that contribute to public health awareness and responsible use of technology.
