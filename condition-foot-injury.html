Foot Injury

Description: A foot injury involves damage to the bones, muscles, tendons, or ligaments of the foot.

Common Causes: Sprains, fractures, overuse, dropping heavy objects, sports activity.

Symptoms: Pain, swelling, bruising, difficulty walking, tenderness to touch.

Treatment: RICE method (Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation), pain relievers, cast or splint for fractures.

When to See a Doctor: If you cannot bear weight, have severe swelling, or suspect a fracture.

Prevention: Wear supportive footwear, warm up before exercise, avoid uneven surfaces.
