Cuts

Description: A cut is a break or opening in the skin caused by a sharp object or impact.

Common Causes: Knives, scissors, broken glass, sharp tools, falls, or accidents.

Symptoms: Bleeding, pain, redness, swelling, tenderness, and possible infection.

Treatment: Clean the wound with water, apply antiseptic, stop bleeding with pressure, and cover with a clean bandage.

When to See a Doctor: If the cut is deep, bleeding heavily, shows signs of infection, or does not heal properly.

Prevention: Handle sharp objects carefully, wear protective gear, and keep dangerous tools out of reach.
