Head Injury

Description: A head injury is any trauma to the scalp, skull, or brain ranging from a mild bump to a severe traumatic brain injury.

Common Causes: Falls, vehicle accidents, sports injuries, physical assault, workplace accidents.

Symptoms: Headache, dizziness, confusion, nausea, loss of consciousness, memory problems.

Treatment: Rest, ice pack, pain relievers, immediate medical care for severe cases, possible surgery.

When to See a Doctor: Immediately if there is loss of consciousness, seizures, repeated vomiting, or worsening headache.

Prevention: Wear helmets during sports and cycling, use seatbelts, remove fall hazards at home.
