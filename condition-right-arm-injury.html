Right Arm Injury

Description: A right arm injury involves damage to the muscles, bones, joints, or tendons of the right arm.

Common Causes: Falls, sports impact, overuse, fractures, dislocations, muscle strains.

Symptoms: Pain, swelling, limited range of motion, bruising, numbness or tingling.

Treatment: Rest, ice, compression, elevation, sling for support, physical therapy, surgery if severe.

When to See a Doctor: If arm is visibly deformed, severely swollen, or you cannot move it.

Prevention: Stretch before physical activity, use proper technique in sports, wear protective gear.
