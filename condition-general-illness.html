General Illness

Description: General illness refers to a state of poor health affecting the body, often with multiple overlapping symptoms.

Common Causes: Viral or bacterial infections, fatigue, poor nutrition, stress, environmental factors.

Symptoms: Fatigue, fever, body aches, loss of appetite, weakness, headache.

Treatment: Rest, hydration, over-the-counter medication, balanced diet, medical consultation if needed.

When to See a Doctor: If symptoms are severe, persistent beyond a week, or rapidly worsening.

Prevention: Maintain good hygiene, eat well, exercise regularly, get adequate sleep.
