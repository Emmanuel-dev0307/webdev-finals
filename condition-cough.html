Cough

Description: A cough is a reflex action to clear the airways of mucus, irritants, or foreign particles.

Common Causes: Cold, flu, asthma, allergies, acid reflux, infections.

Symptoms: Persistent throat clearing, dry or wet cough, chest discomfort, sore throat.

Treatment: Cough syrup, honey and warm water, steam inhalation, antibiotics if bacterial.

When to See a Doctor: If cough lasts more than 3 weeks, produces blood, or is accompanied by chest pain.

Prevention: Avoid smoking, stay hydrated, cover mouth when coughing, wash hands regularly.
