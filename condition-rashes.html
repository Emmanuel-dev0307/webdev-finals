Rashes

Description: A rash is a noticeable change in the texture or color of the skin, often causing irritation or itching.

Common Causes: Allergies, eczema, contact dermatitis, infections, heat, insect bites.

Symptoms: Red or discolored patches, itching, swelling, bumps, dry or scaly skin.

Treatment: Hydrocortisone cream, antihistamines, avoid irritants, keep skin moisturized.

When to See a Doctor: If rash spreads rapidly, is painful, blisters, or is accompanied by fever.

Prevention: Avoid known allergens, use gentle skin products, keep skin clean and dry.
